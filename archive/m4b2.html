Battle 2: Waaagh! Krall versus Crusade Castigatus

The Black Templars were securing their garrisons on Gnost when Waaagh! Krall krooz'd through their territory in their souped-up Trukks and a Wagon load of Burna Boyz.

The patrolling scouts in the area knew their sniper rifles would prove ineffectual against the heavy armor and the Orks' superior numbers. They called in heavy support, and got a response from their Master of the Forge. He brought his personal Land Raider Redeemer, well suited for dealing with Orks.

In the end, the flamestorm cannons ignited the burna boyz' promethium tanks, and burned to the Orks to ashes, resulting in a completely secured planet for the Black Templars.
