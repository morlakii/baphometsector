General Note

These bonuses are intended to be temporary boons, which help losing players come back and reverse the runaway trajectory of the campaign. I reserve the right to make balance changes to buff/nerf some bonuses as needed throughout the campaign.

Basic Rules

  • Once you've been matched with an opponent, but before Selecting a Mission to play, count the number of territories that you and your opponent control.
  • If you control fewer territories than your opponent, select a number of bonuses equal to the difference in controlled territories between you and your opponent. The bonuses are selected from the territories that you own, or from the bonus table below.
    • For example: Player A controls 3 territories, and Player B controls 5 territories. Player A chooses 1 bonus from the table below, and 1 from a territory that he owns.
  • Any Bonus Selections must be used in the upcoming game. If they are not used by the end of the game, they are lost.
  • You must disclose any Bonus Selections to your opponent before the start of the game.
  • Some bonuses will require the Player to own certain territories in order to activate.
    • Example: The orbital bombardment requires the Player to control the Orbital Shipyard.

Bonus Types

  • Territory Bonuses may only be selected if you currently control the corresponding territory, located on the map.
  • For example, the Orbital Bombardment bonus requires the player to control the Baphomet Prime Sector 2.
  • Tactical Bonuses may be selected by any player, regardless of territory control.
  • No bonus may be selected more than once.

Territory Required

    See the entry in the Sector Map.

No requirements

  • Solar Eclipse
    • The night fighting rules are in effect for this game turn.
  • Battle Experience
    • Your warlord may take one additional warlord trait of your choice from any warlord trait table normally available.
  • Heightened Stakes
    • If you win this game, invade an extra territory from the invaded player.
  • All According to Plan
    • Successfully seize the initiative on a roll of 2+ instead of on a roll of 6.
  • Desperate Measures
    • One infantry unit gains the Feel No Pain and Fearless special rules for this game.
  • Unwavering Assault
    • One infantry unit gains the Furious Charge and Relentless for this game.
  • Operation Midnight
    • One infantry unit gains the Stealth and Deepstrike rules for this game.
  • Guerilla Fighting
    • One infantry unit gains the Hit & Run and Infiltrate rules for this game.
